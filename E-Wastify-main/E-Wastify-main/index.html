REDUCE


Reducing resource consumption increases the amount of resources available for product production and utilisation. This may be done by integrating technology into the production process, which will increase efficiency and lower gearbox loss. In order to remove and prepare usable resources for reuse, this also applies to waste minimization.

REUSE


The cycle of reuse is multidirectional, involving the extraction of materials for fresh production when a product loses utility and nears end of life. Optimal resource utilization leads to better growth and reduced waste, especially in small products like metal or plastic plates or sheets.

RECYCLE


When a product loses utility and is getting close to its end of life, the cycle of reuse is multidirectional and involves the extraction of resources for new manufacturing. Better development and less waste result from optimal resource utilisation, especially for tiny goods like plates or sheets made of metal or plastic.
